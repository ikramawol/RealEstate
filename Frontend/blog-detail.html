Top Factors to Consider Before Buying a Home

Home Buying & Selling Tips • 6 min read

Essential Steps to Take Before Making an Offer on a Home.

Selam Getahun

Property Manager
Mar 15, 2025 • 18 Comments

Buying a home is more than just choosing a place to live.it's a major financial and emotional decision. Before signing any papers, it’s essential to consider several key factors such as location, neighborhood safety, access to services, and long-term growth potential. These elements not only affect your day-to-day life but also impact the future resale value of your property.

In this blog, we explore the top things every buyer should evaluate before making a purchase, including budgeting, property condition, legal checks, and selecting the right home type. Whether you're a first-time buyer or looking to invest in Ethiopia’s growing real estate market, this guide offers practical insights to help you make a smart and confident decision.
